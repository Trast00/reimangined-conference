GLOBAL SUMMIT
2015

A joyful celebration believing in the value of openness sharing, creating a positive change with from over 80 countries is taking place In October In Korea

2015.10.15(THU) ~ 16 (FRI)

@ National Museum of Korea, Art Center and more

Main Program

  • Lecture

    Listen to the speakers from various countries about the messeges of sharing and opening

  • CC Exhibition

    Appreciate various creations applying CC licence of artists, organized from Art Center Nabi

  • Forum

    Have the time to share your thoughts and opinions with experts for each topic

  • Workshop

    Try creating your own work using open source licence rather than just watching

  • CC Ignite

    Get opportunities to network with CC affiliates around the world, also after the summit
